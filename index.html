  • sports

    The school identifies excellent performers in each discipline with best sports man award and honors them during annual sports day. To encourage budding talents, promising player in each discipline are awarded trophies. Individual champion in each category in the annual athletics meet is awarded with trophy.

  • inter house

    Inter house sports competitions are being held in every sports discipline. The students are divided into 4 houses.The competitions are held for sub-junior, junior, senior and super senior categories for boys and girls seperately . The competition culminates with the annual sports day. All children are encouraged to participate in various competitions held throughout the year for their house. This system ensures that no child is left without a competition's exposure.
